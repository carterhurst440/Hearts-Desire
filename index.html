How to play

  • Place your wager, then start the hand to let the dealer draw cards.
  • Hearts build your payout ladder. After each heart, choose to withdraw or press.
  • Clubs and diamonds are neutral. Four neutrals end the hand with a loss.
  • Any spade immediately loses the bet. The queen of hearts instantly pays 2:1.
  • Capture five hearts to claim the max 100:1 payout.
